# Privacy Policy for Ultimate Envelopes

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 5th, 2026

Ultimate Envelopes ("the App") is a comprehensive envelope budgeting system built on Google Sheets that leverages Tiller for data aggregation. Your privacy is important, and this Privacy Policy explains what information is (and is not) collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## 1. Information We Collect

Ultimate Envelopes collects minimal information necessary to provide its functionality.

### Information Collected:

**Email address**  
Your Google account email address is used solely to identify you as a user of the app and to enable the app to function properly within your Google account.

### Information NOT Collected:

- No bank account numbers or credentials (Tiller handles bank connections separately)
- No personal contact information beyond email
- No spreadsheet contents stored externally
- No personal files or documents
- No usage analytics beyond what Google provides by default

**The app does not collect, store, or transmit the contents of your Google Sheets to external servers.**

## 2. How Information Is Used

The email address collected is used only for:

- Identifying the user within the app
- Ensuring proper app functionality and permissions

The information is **not** used for:

- Advertising
- Marketing
- Tracking
- Analytics outside of Google's standard platform tools

## 3. Data Protection and Security Measures

Ultimate Envelopes accesses sensitive financial data (transaction amounts, merchant names, account balances) to provide envelope budgeting functionality. We protect this data through the following mechanisms:

### Technical Safeguards

**Client-Side Processing Only**
- All financial data processing occurs entirely within your browser and Google Sheets
- No financial data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services
- Calculations and budget operations happen in real-time within your spreadsheet

**No External Data Storage**
- We do not store, cache, or retain your financial data on any external servers
- Transaction data, account balances, and budget information remain exclusively in your Google Sheets
- We do not maintain databases or data warehouses containing user financial information

**Google's Infrastructure Security**
- All data remains within Google's secure cloud infrastructure
- Data is protected by Google's enterprise-grade security measures
- Access is controlled through Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication system

### Access Controls

**OAuth 2.0 Authorization**
- Users must explicitly grant permission for the add-on to access their spreadsheet
- Authorization can be revoked at any time through Google Account settings
- Permissions are scoped to only what is necessary for functionality

**User-Controlled Access**
- Users maintain full control over their spreadsheet data
- Users can remove the add-on at any time
- Removing the add-on immediately terminates all data access

**No Third-Party Sharing**
- Financial data is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties
- We do not sell, rent, or license user financial information
- No financial data is used for marketing or analytics purposes

### Data Minimization

**Limited Data Access**
- We access only the specific data columns necessary for budgeting: dates, merchants, amounts, and categories
- We do not access bank account numbers, passwords, or login credentials
- We do not read data from unrelated tabs or sheets within your spreadsheet

**No Data Retention**
- Since we do not store data externally, there is no data retention period
- All financial information exists only in your Google Sheets
- Deleting your spreadsheet permanently removes all associated data

### Encryption

**Data in Transit**
- All communication between your browser and Google's servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- API calls to Google Sheets are encrypted end-to-end

**Data at Rest**
- Your spreadsheet data is encrypted at rest by Google's infrastructure
- Google employs industry-standard encryption for stored data

### Compliance

**Google API Services User Data Policy**
- Our use of Google API data adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including Limited Use requirements
- Financial data accessed from Google Sheets is used solely to provide budgeting functionality
- We comply with all restrictions on using, storing, or sharing Google user data

**No Secondary Use**
- Financial data is not used for any purpose other than providing the core add-on functionality
- Data is not used to train machine learning models
- Data is not used for research or analytics

### Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security concern:
- Users can immediately revoke access through Google Account settings at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
- Users can contact us at security@ultimateenvelopes.com
- We will respond to security inquiries within 48 hours

### User Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access your data** - It's in your Google Sheet; you always have access
- **Delete your data** - Delete your spreadsheet or uninstall the add-on
- **Revoke authorization** - Through Google Account permissions at any time
- **Export your data** - Use Google Sheets' built-in export features (File → Download)

### Audit and Monitoring

- We regularly review our security practices
- We monitor for unauthorized access attempts
- We maintain compliance with Google's security requirements

## 4. Data Access and Storage

- Ultimate Envelopes does not store user data on external servers
- All budgeting data remains entirely within the user's own Google Sheets
- The developer does not have access to user spreadsheet data
- All processing occurs within your browser and Google's secure infrastructure

## 5. Data Sharing and Selling

- User data is **never sold, rented, or shared** with third parties
- No data is shared with advertisers or analytics providers
- No data is transferred outside of Google's ecosystem
- Your financial information remains completely private

## 6. Google API Services Compliance

Ultimate Envelopes' use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

User data is accessed only to provide the app's core functionality and for no other purpose.

## 7. Tiller Money Integration

**Important:** Ultimate Envelopes integrates with Tiller Money to access transaction data that Tiller aggregates from your financial institutions.

- **Tiller Money is a separate service** with its own privacy policy
- Ultimate Envelopes reads transaction data that Tiller has already placed in your Google Sheets
- We do not have direct access to your bank accounts
- All bank connections are managed by Tiller, not Ultimate Envelopes
- Please review [Tiller's Privacy Policy](https://www.tillerhq.com/privacy-policy/) for information about how Tiller handles your financial data

## 8. User Control and Consent

- You may revoke the app's access at any time through your [Google Account permissions page](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
- Removing access immediately stops all interaction between the app and your account
- You can uninstall the add-on at any time from the Extensions menu in Google Sheets
- Your spreadsheet data remains yours after uninstalling

## 9. Children's Privacy

Ultimate Envelopes is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. 

We will notify users of material changes through:
- An update notification in the add-on
- A notice on our website

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
